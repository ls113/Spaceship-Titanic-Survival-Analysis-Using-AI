About the Mission

The Spaceship Titanic was a luxury interstellar liner carrying almost 13,000 passengers on its maiden voyage from Earth to three newly habitable exoplanets.

Project Objective

Using the latest advances in Random Forest Classification, this AI suite analyzes passenger spending patterns, cryogenic status, and cabin assignments to predict who was transported to an alternate dimension after the anomaly near 55 Cancri e.

Tech Stack
  • Backend: Python / Flask
  • Model: Scikit-Learn (Random Forest)
  • Explainability: SHAP (Shapley Additive Explanations)
  • UI: Bootstrap 5 & Jinja2 Templates
